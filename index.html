Official Guidelines (2026)

U UPSC Application Requirements

📷 Photograph Specifications

  • • Dimensions: 4 cm × 5 cm (approximately 472×591 pixels at 300 DPI)
  • • File Size: Between 20 KB and 300 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG only
  • • Face Coverage: 50% of image area
  • • Background: Plain white or light
  • • Watermark: Name & Date required (2026)

✍️ Signature Specifications

  • • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm (approximately 413×177 pixels)
  • • File Size: Between 20 KB and 50 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG only
  • • Background: White/transparent

I IIT-JEE Application Requirements

📷 Photograph Specifications

  • • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm (approximately 413×531 pixels)
  • • File Size: Between 10 KB and 200 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG only
  • • Face Coverage: 50% of image area
  • • Background: Plain white or light
  • • Recent Photo: Preferably taken within 6 months

✍️ Signature Specifications

  • • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm (approximately 413×177 pixels)
  • • File Size: Between 10 KB and 100 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG only
  • • Background: White

N NEET (NTA) Application Requirements

📷 Passport Size Photograph

  • • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm (~413×531 px)
  • • File Size: 10 KB - 200 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG

🖼️ Postcard Size Photograph

  • • Dimensions: 4 × 6 inches (~472×708 px)
  • • File Size: 10 KB - 200 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG

✍️ Signature

  • • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm (~413×177 px)
  • • File Size: 4 KB - 30 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG

👍 Thumb Impression

  • • Aspect Ratio: Standard thumb (approx 2:2)
  • • File Size: 10 KB - 200 KB
  • • Format: JPG/JPEG
